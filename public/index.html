Time to get organized

Finance Management system will help you organize all of your financial dues, in just a few clicks. Just login with your account and you can track all the bills you have to pay for the month. With the help of this app, your budgetary control will be easier than ever.

About us

A Finance and Bill Management App that reminds its users via notifications or email about their upcoming due dates for bills and subscriptions. The web portal can provide information on total expenses, frequency of transactions, and its current status whether paid or unpaid. It highlights urgent bills that are in need of payment and will make the user know when the bill is due to be paid.

The application was built as a main requirement for the subject CMSC 173, Institute of Computer Science, University of the Philippines Los Banos. The developer team is composed of Junior Level UPLB students that major in Computer Science, and each of their info can be found at the bottom of the page.
